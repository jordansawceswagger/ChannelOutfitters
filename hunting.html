Hunting

We offer trophy elk, mule and whitetail deer, and pronghorn antelope hunts on large private ranches in eastern Montana, near Malta, Miles City, the Powder River, and the Bull Mountains. Through stringent management goals, these fair chase hunts are of the highest standards for rifle and archery hunts, alike.

Channel Outfitters works closely with clients to customize and plan a hunting experience to create lasting memories. No two hunting trips are alike. Call or email us to get started planning yours.

Elk

Eastern Montana's Bull Mountains are renowned as home to one of the highest populations of trophy quality elk. Our well-managed ranches produce a high success rate for elk harvesting. We hunt on moderate terrain, which makes for easy accessibility. We'll provide a challenging and rewarding experience for both rifle and archery hunts. Elk archery season opens the first weekend in September; rifle season runs mid-October through late November.

Mule Deer & Whitetail Deer

Eastern Montana is home to some of the best mule deer habitats in North America. We have exclusive leases on excellent properties, which are strictly managed to produce trophy quality mule deer. And while Montana may not be known for whitetail, our management practices produce a healthy population of this majestic animal. Montana's bow season runs weeks prior to rifle season, and rifle season runs through the entire deer rut. Many of our bigger trophies are harvested during this time.

Pronghorn Antelope

The antelope hunt is one of the most action-packed and affordable hunts. It's common to see more than 100 antelope a day on most of our ranches. Montana antelope bow hunts run through the rut in September. Rifle season runs October and November. Antelope hunts are easily combined with all other hunts.

Licensing

We can assist clients on all licensing matters, helping navigate Montana Fish, Wildlife & Parks tag draw process.

Lodging

Channel Outfitters is pleased to offer accommodations within all our hunting packages. One less thing for you to worry about. Our historic lodge is situated in the Bull Mountains near Musselshell, Mont. Back in the day, it was an old storefront. Today, it has been transformed into a rustic yet comfortable hunting lodge that sleeps six with its two bedrooms, one bathroom/two showers, full kitchen, bar, and living/game area. We can also arrange accommodations in local motels and ranch houses.
