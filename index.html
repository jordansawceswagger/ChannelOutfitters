Why Channel Outfitters?

When it comes to fly fishing, the mighty Missouri River in Montana is the be-all and end-all...fly fishing heaven on earth...the ultimate experience for seasoned anglers and first-timers alike. Few know it better than Channel Outfitters. It's what we do. Day in. Day out. Year after year. For nearly 25 years. It never gets old.

The mighty Mo' boasts more than 5,000 fish per mile. Thick hatches of all the right bugs at all the right times. Stunning scenery on Lewis & Clark's historic trail. Call, text, or email us now to inquire more or book your trip.

Need more reasons? Don't listen to us. Clients' smiles say it all.

Guided Fly Fishing

Missouri River · Since 1998

Channel Outfitters has been offering guided fly fishing trips throughout Montana since 1998. After bouncing around on most rivers in western Montana we have narrowed it down to Montana's Missouri River where fly fishing is simply the best!

Full and half-day drift boat trips. All gear, flies, lunch and local transport included. First-timers and experienced anglers welcome.

Trophy Hunting

Eastern Montana · Private Ranches

We offer trophy elk, mule and whitetail deer, and pronghorn antelope hunts on large private ranches in eastern Montana, near Malta, Miles City, the Powder River, and the Bull Mountains.

Through stringent management goals, these fair chase hunts are of the highest standards for rifle and archery hunts, alike. No two hunting trips are alike.
